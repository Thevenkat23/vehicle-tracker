Questions

Before you install.

Is it really free?

Yes. No trial period, no subscription, no locked features and no advertising. It's a tool, not a business model.

Does it work without internet?

Completely. Everything runs on your phone, including the bill scanning. There is no server to connect to.

How many vehicles can I add?

As many as you own. Bikes, scooters, cars and EVs can all sit in the same garage.

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. We never receive it and have no way to see it. Read the privacy policy for the detail.

Can I back it up?

Yes — export a file at any time and keep it wherever you like. If Android's system backup is switched on, your data can also restore automatically to a new phone through your own Google account.

Does it support electric vehicles?

Yes. EVs log charging in kWh instead of litres, and the app works out km per kWh and your running cost the same way.

Will there be an iPhone version?

It's planned, but there's no date yet. Android first.

🔒

Your records stay on your phone

There's no account to create and no server to sign in to. Bill photos are read on the device itself. Back up whenever you like — to a file you keep, or to your own Google Drive. Read the privacy policy.
