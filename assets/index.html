Three numbers most people never work out.

Your app store is full of expense trackers. This one knows what a vehicle actually is: a machine that turns rupees into kilometres.

₹2.40 /km
What it truly costs to run — fuel and servicing, divided by the distance you actually covered.
38.5 km/L
Real mileage, measured between full tanks. Not the figure on the brochure.
67 days
Until the next oil change — counted down from your live odometer, not a fixed date.

What it keeps, so you don't have to.

Fuel
Every fill-up, in three tapsTap ₹500, pick your pump, done. It remembers your last price and estimates the odometer for you. Mileage is calculated only between full tanks, so the number means something.
Oil
Counts down as you rideSet it once at 16,000 km with a 2,000 km interval. Every time you log fuel, the countdown updates from your real odometer.
Service
Every bill, every rupeeWhat was done, what it cost, which garage. Set a schedule by months or kilometres — whichever comes first.
Papers
RC, PUC and insuranceStore the numbers and expiry dates. The app warns you a week before, so you don't find out at a checkpost.
Trips
Where you went, what it costRoute, stops, distance — plus fuel, tolls, food and everything else, totalled per trip.
Garage
Bike, scooter, car or EVPetrol, diesel, CNG, hybrid or electric. EVs log charging in kWh and get km/kWh instead of km/L.
